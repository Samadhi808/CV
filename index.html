Michael Underwood

I have been working in the printing industry for 30 years. I have mainly worked in the bindery department running various machines. I am more skilled in folders but I do run paper cutters, saddle-stitchers trimmers. I have even have several years in the mailing department as well running inserters and inkjets.

I began in the printing industry in the summer of 1989. I worked at Ross Printing & Marketing as a cutter operator for the first year then I was asked if I wanted to work on a Didde Forms Web Press. I did this for another year and had worked my way up to second pressman.

While working there, I worked with a bindery person who was an expert at running the bindery machines. I told myself that I wanted to be as good as he was but I wanted to know more. Now that I have come to the 30 year mark in this career, I have completed what I wanted to do with these skills.
