Michael Underwood

I have been working in the printing industry as a bindery machine operator for 30 years running various machines and handwork projects. Folders is the main skill set however, I do run paper cutters, saddle-stitchers trimmers as well. I have spent several years running inserters and inkjets within the mailing department.

I began in the printing industry in the summer of 1989. I started as a cutter operator for the first year then moved to the Didde Forms Web-Press working as a presshelper for about 4 months then worked my way up to second pressman.

While working there, I worked with a man who was an expert at running the bindery machines. I told myself I wanted to be as good, if not better as he was. Now that I have come to the 30 year mark in my career, I completed what I wanted to do by learning most of the skills needed to run the bindery..

Throughout the years, I have maintained a bindery machine operator position while guiding others as a lead and/or supervisor position.
